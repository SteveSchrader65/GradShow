Grad Show 2024 recognises Australian Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Peoples unique cultural and spiritual relationships to the land, waters and seas and their rich contribution to society. We acknowledge the Noongar People, the traditional custodians of the lands on which our campuses are located and pay our respects to ancestors and Elders past and present.

We support the Uluru Statement from the Heart and embrace the invitation to walk alongside Aboriginal people, in a movement for a better future for all Australians.
